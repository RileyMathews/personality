INFP: The Mediator

Facts about the Mediator

  1. They have a few trusted close friends, though they can be difficult to get to know.
  2. They can be idealistic to a fault.
  3. They seek value and harmony, and dislike when people try to dominate a situation.
  4. They can work through hardships because they see the end goal, and have a vision to keep them going.
  5. They can take criticism personaly, and will sometimes spend time trying to find a middle ground with the criticism.
